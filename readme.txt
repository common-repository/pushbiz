== Description ==
**PushBIZ Push Notification?**

>PushBIZ [push notifications](https://www.pushbiz.in/pushbizhome) lets you notify a user of new messages or events  even when the user is not using your website. When a user device receives a [push notification](https://www.pushbiz.in/pushbizhome), your website's icon and a message appear in the user screen. User can click on the message and can see more details, which you wish him to see, by clicking on the message. With pushbiz [push notifications](https://www.pushbiz.in/pushbizhome), you do not have to wait for your users to open their inbox to communicate. It will be delivered right on their browser. [PushBiz](https://www.pushbiz.in/pushbizhome) lets you send [push notifications](https://www.pushbiz.in/pushbizhome) on your subscribers via API too. You can choose events in your ERP when you wish to notify users. [PushBIZ](https://www.pushbiz.in/pushbizhome) also allows segmenting the users based on geo coordinates, state, city, role etc and can send [push notifications](https://www.pushbiz.in/pushbizhome) only for selected segments. PushBIZ [push notification](https://www.pushbiz.in/pushbizhome) make your business visible to your clients and thus enables you earn upto 10X more.

Installing the WordPress [PushBIZ](https://www.pushbiz.in/pushbizhome) plugin will automatically add the needed [PushBIZ](https://www.pushbiz.in/pushbizhome) Code on your WordPress website. To get started, you just have to share your phone no, website domain and email ID. Once Configurations are down, just push your business near to your client from with-in Wordpress, using **Send BIZ Message**.
